ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Cat Kenzie! I’m a Firmware Engineer who recently relocated to the San Jose area. I’m excited by facing new challenges, connecting with people, and discovering more about the world each day.

The world makes sense to me when I have a deep understanding of how things operate, so I appreciate the low level approach firmware engineering takes to solve problems.

My studies focused on embedded systems, signal processing, and control systems. I have experience collaboratively designing and developing embedded systems using C, Verilog HDL, ARM and Python to control ADC/DACs, motors, ultrasonic sensors, speakers, resistive touchscreens, among other peripherals.

Currently, I work extensively using bash commands and postgreSQL queries and I've been learning the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in my free time.

I’m looking for a role to use my toolbox of skills to create specialized technological solutions that benefit others and elevate the collective. When I am away from a screen you will find me learning skateboard tricks, testing out vegetarian recipes, and sharing my love of electronic music with others.
